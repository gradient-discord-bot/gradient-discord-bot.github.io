Configuration

A customizable bot is a good bot. Gradient is equipped with a suite of user settings for you to tune to your liking, and server settings that enable advanced functionality for all members.

Economy

Ever wanted to get free money every day, rob your friends, or reach the apex of wealth? With Gradient's robust fictitional currency system, coins, you can achieve all of this and much more.

Fun

These random fun and memey commands are guranteed to put a smile on your face and add some spice to conversations in your server.

Games

Pass some time or take a little break with Gradient's wide selection of minigames that you can play directly within your server! Test your sports skills, revisit classics such as Battleship and Yahtzee, challenge a friend to Tic Tac Toe or Uno, and more!

Images

Add hilarious effects to a member's profile picture, create your own memes from templates, and unleash creativity with AI-powered image generation with Gradient's collection of image manipulation commands.

Moderation

Ever wanted to be able to modify server components and moderate members with a quick command? Gradient has you covered, and you can even enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) to add an extra layer of security.

Music

Start a listening party directly within a voice channel in your server, and control what's playing with an advanced queue system.

Sciences

Satisfy your inner nerd, or get homework help instantly with Gradient's advanced scientific lookup and mathematical computation commands, including step-by-step solutions from WolframAlpha.

Statistics

Gradient easily and aesthetically displays detailed stats for your server, fetches up-to-date data for games such as League of Legends and Supercell games, and looks up real-world information such as Google Maps and public transit.

Text

Tired of having to open a separate website to generate some superscript, convert some text into binary, or look up the definition of a word? With Gradient, you can now work with text directly within Discord like never before.

Utility

Gradient's utility commands provide general helpful functions such as checking the bot's status, creating a QR code, and even searching Google!

Premium

Gradient is completely free to use and always will be. Users who meet the Premium requirements are eligible to receive Gradient Premium at no cost and take the Gradient experience to the next level.
